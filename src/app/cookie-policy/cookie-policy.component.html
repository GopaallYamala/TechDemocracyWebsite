Cookie Policy

Welcome to TechDemocracy! This Cookie Policy elucidates how we utilize cookies and comparable tracking technologies when you access and use our website.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They serve multiple purposes, including enhancing your browsing experience and enabling us to understand how you interact with our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies:

These cookies are fundamental for the website to function effectively. They enable basic functionalities and ensure the security of our platform. Without these cookies, certain features may not be accessible.

Analytical/Performance Cookies:

We utilize these cookies to gather information about visitors' browsing habits on our website. This data helps us improve user experience, identify areas for enhancement, and understand user preferences. It's anonymized and used for statistical analysis only.

Functional Cookies:

Functional cookies allow the website to remember your preferences, such as language settings and login details. They help personalize your experience and enable enhanced features tailored to your needs.

How We Use Cookies

Improving User Experience: Cookies help us personalize your browsing experience and provide you with relevant content and services.

Tracking Performance:

They assist us in analyzing website traffic, identifying popular pages, and gauging user engagement to enhance our content and services. Enhancing Security: Cookies contribute to maintaining a secure online environment by identifying and preventing potential security risks.

Managing Cookies

By using TechDemocracy website, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. However, you can manage or delete cookies via your browser settings. Please note that restricting cookies may impact the functionality of certain website features.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We prioritize your privacy and data security. We do not utilize cookies to collect personally identifiable information without your explicit consent. For further information on how we handle your data, please refer to TechDemocracy Privacy Policy.
