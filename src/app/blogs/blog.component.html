Introduction

Introduction In the age of digital transformation, the healthcare industry is rapidly evolving. Electronic health records (EHRs), telemedicine, and mobile healthcare apps are just a few examples of how technology is improving patient care and data accessibility. However, these innovations also bring new cybersecurity challenges, making identity governance and administration (IGA) essential for safeguarding sensitive patient information.

The Importance of IGA in Healthcare:

Protecting patient data:

Patient data is a prime target for cybercriminals due to its high value on the black market. Implementing IGA helps healthcare organizations ensure that only authorized personnel can access sensitive patient records. This mitigates the risk of data breaches and maintains patient confidentiality.

Regulatory Compliance:

The healthcare industry is subject to a multitude of regulations, such as HIPAA in the United States. and GDPR in Europe. Failure to comply can result in severe penalties. IGA solutions assist healthcare organizations in adhering to these regulations by enforcing access controls, auditing, and reporting.
